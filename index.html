Welcome to the UNL Game Development Club

We are UNL's only club focused on learning to develop video games. All are welcome and there are no pre-requisites to join. Visit the contact page to get in touch with us or to join the Discord community.

Our mission: To guide students in learning effective professional game design techniques. We accomplish this by working through game development projects in our meetings, such as demos or active projects. We also provide students the opportunity to showcase their skills at game development competitions ("game jams").

To get involved, please visit the contact page and join the Discord or reach out to one of the club officers.

Upcoming Events

The UNL Game Development Club will meet online on Sundays at 7PM Central for the foreseeable future per request of the UNL Computer Science department. If you are interested in participating, please join the Discord on the contact page.

Game Development Basics (September 13th)

Join us on Sunday, September 13th as we walk through how to do basic 3D modeling in Blender and how to make a basic 3D ball-rolling platformer in Unity! This meeting is intended for anyone of any skill level. If you would like to follow along with the tutorial, please download the latest version of Unity and Blender. After the tutorials in the first hour of the meeting, we will have our first team game development time until 9 PM Central. This meeting will be livestreamed from the Voice: General voice chat of our Discord server. Please join the Discord server at 7 PM Central if you would like to participate!
