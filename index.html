Welcome to the UNL Game Development Club

We are UNL's only club focused on learning to develop video games. All are welcome and there are no pre-requisites to join. Visit the contact page to get in touch with us or to join the Discord community.

Our mission: To guide students in learning effective professional game design techniques. We accomplish this by working through game development projects in our meetings, such as demos or active projects. We also provide students the opportunity to showcase their skills at game development competitions ("game jams").

To get involved, please visit the contact page and join the Discord or reach out to one of the club officers.

Upcoming Events

The UNL Game Development Club will meet online on Sundays at 7PM Central for the foreseeable future per request of the UNL Computer Science department. If you are interested in participating, please join the Discord on the contact page.

Information Meeting (February 21st, 6PM Central)

Join us on Sunday, February 21st at 6PM as we provide information on what the UNL Game Development Club is all about! How to Design Games (February 21st, 7PM Central)

Join us after the information meeting on Sunday, February 21st at 7PM as we practice designing and pitching games! We will do several game design activities to prepare students to pitch games. Please join the Discord server at 7 PM Central if you would like to participate!

Game Pitches and Team Organization (February 28th)

Join us on Sunday, February 28th at 7PM as students pitch their games and teams are organized! First, team leads will be announced. Then, students who submitted a game pitch will be able to pitch it to the club. Finally, the team leads will select which game they would like to develop from the pitches and teams will be organized. This meeting will be held on our Discord server in the General voice channel. Please join the Discord server at 7 PM Central if you would like to participate!
